1) Visual Clarity & Structure

Always aim to keep your site visually clear and well-structured. Proper spacing, alignment, and consistent styles help make your content more readable and professional.

Image / Figure

Alignment example: media block keeps text and image aligned with consistent gaps.

Practical Checklist

  • Use whitespace: apply consistent margins and padding.
  • Align content: headings, paragraphs, and media on the same grid.
  • Keep styles consistent: same fonts, sizes, and line spacing.
  • Limit visual noise: 2–3 fonts and 2–3 main colors.
  • Use semantic HTML: <header>, <main>, <section>, <figure>.

2) Colors — Purposeful & Accessible
